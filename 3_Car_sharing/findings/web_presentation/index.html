Articonf 2022 Datathon
Data set "Car sharing" | Analyzed by Bence Balazs & Benjamin Kampfer

The aim of this analysis is to analyse and visualise the data regarding:

Visualize of communities

Analyze and Visualize which communities can be of utmost importance for business productivity of car-sharing use case

Increase the ROI for carsharing companies and for private vehicle owners

Classify users and set strategies to target users

Relation between the vehicle (model, colour, year and other attributes) and demand in a map

Heat zones of vehicles and users with a higher reputation (for new drivers and passengers)
